Rules

  • Start with 6 dice, roll and check for single 1's and 5's, three or more-of-a-kinds or straights.
  • Hold any scoring dice you want to save. Reroll the remaining dice to try and score more during the round.
  • End your turn when you choose to stop and add the points to your total. If all dice are used to score, the players turn contiues and he may pick up all 6 dice and keep rolling adding points to your total. If none of your dice can score on a roll, your turn ends and you score 0 for the round.
  • You must roll at least 1000 points on your first turn to keep playing.
  • First player to score 10,000 total points, Wins!
